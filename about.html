About SCD

The Society of Capital Design was founded in 2025 by two students, one focused on economics and one on engineering, on a simple observation: the interesting problems live where the two fields meet. Every bridge is a budget. Every budget builds something.

SCD runs through school chapters across the UAE, each with a named student chapter lead. Our programs, essay competitions, speaker sessions and the SCD Bulletin, all put economics students and engineering students on the same problem.
